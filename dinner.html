Paneer Butter Masala

Paneer Butter Masala is a rich and creamy dish made with paneer (Indian cottage cheese) cooked in a buttery tomato-based gravy. It's a favorite in Indian cuisine and pairs well with naan or rice.

Ingredients

For the Gravy

  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 1 cup onions, finely chopped
  • 2 cups tomatoes, pureed
  • 10 cashews (soaked in water)
  • 1 teaspoon ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 teaspoon cumin seeds
  • 1 teaspoon garam masala
  • ½ teaspoon red chili powder
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh cream for garnish

For Cooking Paneer

  • 250 grams paneer, cubed
  • Water as needed

Instructions

1. Prepare the Gravy:

  • Heat butter in a pan over medium heat. Add cumin seeds and let them splutter.
  • Add chopped onions and sauté until golden brown.
  • Stir in ginger-garlic paste and cook for another minute until fragrant.
  • Add tomato puree and cook until oil starts to separate from the mixture.
  • Blend soaked cashews into a smooth paste and add to the gravy along with red chili powder, garam masala, and salt. Mix well.

2. Cook Paneer:

  • Add cubed paneer to the gravy along with enough water to reach your desired consistency.
  • Simmer for about 5-7 minutes until paneer is heated through.

3. Serve:

  • Garnish with fresh cream before serving hot with naan or rice.

Tips

  • Add More Spice: Increase the amount of red chili powder for a spicier dish.
  • Vegetable Variation: You can add mixed vegetables like peas or bell peppers for added nutrition.
  • Dairy-Free Option: Substitute paneer with tofu for a vegan version of this dish.
